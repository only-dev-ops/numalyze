Composite Trapezoidal

{% endblock %} {% block subtext %} Composite Trapezoidal method is used to evaluate the definite integrals. Since Integration represents area under a curve of \(f(x)\), the trapezoidal method uses this to divide up the total area into N trapzoids (or as I like to call it, Panels). Then we calculate the area of each curve and sum them up.
